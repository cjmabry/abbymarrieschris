Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the wedding?

The wedding is at Abby's parents home on Buck Mountain. The ceremony and reception will take place in the backyard, and the rain plan will take place under a large tent.

What is the dress code?

We invite you to wear garden party attire, below are our wedding colors, if you feel so inclined, feel free to join us in wearing these shades. If you like wearing heels, we recommend block heels and wedges as the ceremony and reception will be on the lawn.

Do I get a plus one?

If we reserved a seat for you and a guest, your invitation will reflect that.

Will veggie-friendly food be served?

Yes, we will be serving veggie, steak, and chicken fajitas and tacos.

Can I take photos?

We have our dream photographers documenting our wedding day. It's really important to us that phones and cameras are not out during the ceremony, especially during the processional. We invite you to take as many photos as you please the rest of the day. The wedding is at Abby's parents home on Buck Mountain. The ceremony and reception will take place in the backyard, and the rain plan will take place under a large tent.

Will there be an open bar?

We will be serving beer, wine, and a signature cocktail.

When does the reception end?

The formal reception events will be over at 9pm, but music and dancing will continue as long as we please.
